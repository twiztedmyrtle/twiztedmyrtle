Get Comfortable Being Uncomfortable

Instead, we hunger to venture outside of our comfort zones for personal growth. We engage in thought provoking conversations that are bold, raw and refreshingly honest. We listen with curiosity, compassion and understanding without judgment. Twizted conversations are an intoxicating cocktail of intense, passion and fun, while being inspiring, eye opening and even life-changing.
